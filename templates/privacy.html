Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 1 October 2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to tools.wikilovesfolklore.org (“we”, “our”, or “us”). We respect the privacy of our users and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. This policy describes the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you visit our website and our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, protecting, and disclosing that information.

2. Information Collection

We use MediaWiki authentication to collect only publicly available information from users. We do not collect any personal or sensitive information beyond what is made publicly available by the users themselves.

3. Use of Information

The information we collect is used to enhance user experience, improve our website, and foster a positive user community. We do not share, sell, or lease personal information about you to any third-parties for their marketing use.

4. Compliance with Laws

Our servers are hosted in India, and thus, we abide by the local laws applicable to data protection and privacy. We operate in compliance with all applicable laws and regulations and expect users to do the same.

In addition to local laws, users must also adhere to the Terms of Use and all other policies set forth by the Wikimedia Foundation. We align our practices with the principles and guidelines provided by the Wikimedia Foundation to ensure the protection of user privacy and data.

6. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your information. However, the security of information transmitted through the internet can never be guaranteed. Any transmission of personal information is at your own risk.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. All changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage users to regularly check our privacy policy for any changes.

8. Contact Information

For any queries or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please contact us at tools@wikilovesfolklore.org

9. Acceptance of Privacy Policy

By using tools.wikilovesfolklore.org, you signify your acceptance of this policy. If you do not agree to this policy, please do not use our website.
